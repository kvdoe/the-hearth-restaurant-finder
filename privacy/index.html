Privacy Policy

FoodQuest ("we," "our," or "us") is a restaurant discovery app. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it. We do not sell your data, show you advertisements, or share your information with any marketing or analytics services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Email address and password — collected only if you choose to create an account. Your password is never stored in plain text; Supabase hashes it using bcrypt before storing.
  • Full name and username — collected only if you create an account. These are required fields during sign-up and are stored in your Supabase user profile.
  • Precise location — only when you explicitly tap "Use My Current Location." We request foreground permission only (not background). Your coordinates are sent to our Vercel API to search for nearby restaurants and are not stored in any database by us.
  • Saved restaurants — the list of restaurants you mark as a favorite. These are stored exclusively on your device using local storage (AsyncStorage). They are never uploaded to our servers or linked to your account.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect background location at any time.
  • We do not collect device identifiers, advertising IDs, or device fingerprints.
  • We do not use any third-party analytics SDK (no Firebase, Mixpanel, Segment, etc.).
  • We do not show ads and do not share any data with advertising networks.
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Email address: to create and authenticate your account. You can use FoodQuest without creating an account.
  • Name and username: to display your name within the app.
  • Location: to search for restaurants near you via the Yelp Fusion API. Queries are routed through our own Vercel backend — your location is not sent directly to Yelp.

4. Data Storage and Security

Account data (email, name, username) is stored in Supabase, a hosted PostgreSQL platform. Supabase enforces row-level security, meaning each user can only access their own data. All data in transit is encrypted with TLS (HTTPS).

Saved restaurants are stored only on your device using AsyncStorage and are never transmitted to any server.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data (email, name, username) at any time by emailing kvchiefs@gmail.com with the subject line "Delete my FoodQuest account." We will complete the deletion within 7 days.

Saved restaurants stored on your device are permanently deleted when you uninstall the app.

6. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase (privacy policy) — authentication and user data storage.
  • Vercel (privacy policy) — serverless API hosting. Vercel retains standard infrastructure logs (IP address, timestamp, request path) as described in their policy. We do not access or use these logs.
  • Yelp Fusion API — restaurant data. Search queries contain a location and cuisine type only; no personal identifiers are forwarded to Yelp.

7. Children's Privacy

FoodQuest is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date below. Your continued use of FoodQuest after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

9. Contact

For questions, privacy requests, or account deletion, email kvchiefs@gmail.com.
